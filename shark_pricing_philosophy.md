SharkAuth Pricing Philosophy
Core Principle
The binary is the product. Cloud is a convenience layer, not a feature gate.
Every auth feature — MFA, SSO, RBAC, audit logs, sessions, migration — ships in the binary and is available to every self-hosted user at $0. This is non-negotiable. The moment you gate features behind cloud, you become Clerk with extra steps.
What Cloud Sells
Cloud doesn't sell features. Cloud sells operational burden:

Managed infrastructure (uptime, backups, scaling)
Dashboard + analytics
SLA guarantees
Priority/dedicated support
Multi-region HA
14-day free trial to evaluate the managed experience

Self-hosted users get the same engine. Cloud users pay to not think about it.

Why This Works
Self-hosted as the free tier solves three problems at once:

Acquisition cost = zero. The binary is the top of funnel. No cloud infra spent on free users who may never convert. Clerk needs a free tier because they have no self-hosted option. We don't.
Every cloud customer is a paying customer. No anchoring users to "$0 cloud." Everyone on cloud has already cleared the willingness-to-pay barrier.
Full feature parity on self-hosted builds trust, not competition. A dev who self-hosts and loves it becomes the internal champion who pushes for cloud when the team grows and ops becomes a headache. Gating features would poison that pipeline.
