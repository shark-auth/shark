## Open source positioning

Open source is the moat for SharkAuth specifically because:
- Trust: auth must be auditable. Closed-source auth at startup scale is a dealbreaker.
- Distribution: open source = MCP server builders can read the code, embed, fork.
- YC angle: hosted/cloud tier is the future revenue path. Self-hosted free is the wedge.

Make this explicit in the YC application: "self-hosted free, hosted/cloud paid is the revenue plan; open source is the wedge for distribution and trust."
