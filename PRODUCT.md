product

## Users
Developers and admins managing authentication and users for their applications.

## Product Purpose
SharkAuth admin dashboard for managing users, emails, settings, and viewing system health.

## Brand Personality
Technical, precise, minimal, professional.

## Anti-references
Overly playful, excessive gradients, confusing layouts.

## Design Principles
Clear data presentation, efficient workflows, high signal-to-noise ratio.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
